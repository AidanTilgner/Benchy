# Profiling Non-Deterministic Performance and Behavior
In a world of autonomous agents who do what they like rather than what instructed, it's important to still track the performance and capabilites of agents over time. Half of improvement is knowing where you stand. Benchy is a library for building dialogical benchmarks for vertical AI agents. It's a tool for profiling non-deterministic performance and behavior.
